Health Tips & Emergency Advice

Stay informed. These tips might save a life.

Don’t Move the Injured

Unless there is a fire or danger, avoid moving accident victims. This prevents spinal injuries from worsening.

Share Exact Location

Use your phone’s GPS to share the exact accident spot. It helps ambulances reach quickly without confusion.

Stop the Bleeding

Use a clean cloth or bandage to apply gentle pressure on bleeding wounds until medical help arrives.

Check for Consciousness

Gently tap the person and call out. If unresponsive, check breathing. This helps responders act faster.

Record the Scene

Capture images or videos of the accident scene. It helps in AI analysis and assists authorities in documentation.

Call Emergency Numbers First

Before anything else, call the emergency number or report using Call4Care. Every second counts in trauma cases.
